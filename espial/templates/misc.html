Create all concept notes

Concept notes are notes that list every document associated with a given concept, allowing Espial's structure to become integrated with your personal KB.

Create all tags

Clicking the button above will integrate each detected tag into your knowledge base. By default this means it replaces occurences of the concept in your notes by #concept, but this is configurable.
